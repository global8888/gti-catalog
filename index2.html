About Us

Global Technology Import-Export Group Co., Ltd. (GTI) is headquartered in Vientiane, Laos, and upholds the mission of "Connecting Global Technology and Regional Development." It aims to become a leading platform for the integration of technology and capital in Southeast Asia.

With core strategic focuses in defense equipment trade, system integration, and mineral resource development, the company leverages its accumulated industry resources and operational excellence over many years to provide government and corporate clients with comprehensive solutions ranging from hardware supply to technical service support. GTI is committed to enhancing regional business capabilities and fostering sustainable prosperity.

Core Strengths

  • Profound Regional Insight: The core team possesses extensive years of experience in the Lao and Southeast Asian markets, with a deep understanding of local policies and cultural requirements.
  • Integrated Service Capability: Capable of providing one-stop integrated solutions from R&D and production, system integration, to logistics, transportation, and after-sales support.
  • Compliance and Sustainable Development: Strictly adheres to international defense trade regulations and regional laws, and is dedicated to implementing the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Future Vision

GTI will continue to expand into ASEAN and global markets, solidifying its competitive edge in the fields of defense equipment, simulation technology, and mineral resource development. Through continuous technological upgrading, regional cooperation, and talent development, GTI aims to become a significant force in promoting Laos's industrial modernization and regional economic cooperation, striving to build a model technology enterprise rooted locally with a global reach.
