Global Technology Import-Export Group Co., Ltd.

1. Defense Equipment Procurement and Technology Integration

GTI is a fully qualified, high-standard defense service provider within Laos. It integrates global advanced technologies and equipment resources to deliver systematic defense support services across a broad spectrum of fields including the Army, Navy, Air Force, Aerospace, and Electronics.

  • Equipment Procurement Agency System: Serving as an agent for major international defense contractors, providing procurement services for items including small arms, armored vehicles, command systems, missiles, and related components.
  • Training and Simulation Systems: Providing training and simulation system solutions for various military branches.
  • System Integration Capability: Offering integration services for systems such as C4ISR, strategic communications, reconnaissance and surveillance, and cyber defense systems.
  • Customized Solutions: Designing and implementing specialized protection systems like regional cybersecurity and coastal defense based on client needs.
  • Comprehensive Service Support: Providing full-process support from equipment maintenance and technical training to upgrades, enhancing troop readiness and self-sustainability.

2. Civil Explosives and Engineering Services

  • Product Supply: Supplying a full range of products including industrial explosives, electronic detonators, and blasting systems.
  • Technical Support and Management: Introducing advanced blasting technologies and traceable management systems to ensure project safety and compliant operations.
  • Plan Design and Guidance: Providing specialized blasting plan design and technical guidance to manage project risks and control operational hazards.

3. Mineral Resource Development and Operations

  • Mineral Resource Development: Conducting environmentally friendly and compliant exploration and development of minerals such as gold, copper, and rare earth elements.
  • Capacity Building: Providing services to improve operational efficiency and skills training, and implementing cooperative projects that promote community development.
  • Professional Platform Development: Establishing reliable channels for professional resources and strengthening core regional mineral development capabilities.

4. Specialized Defense Logistics and Cross-Border Transportation

  • Establishing a Compliant Logistics System: Building an efficient, compliant cargo transportation system with the global capability to handle high-risk defense material shipments.
  • Security Assurance and Global Network: Ensuring secure and direct delivery of equipment to clients and establishing a global support network for complex projects.

Core Strengths

  • Profound Regional Insight: The core team possesses extensive years of experience in the Lao and Southeast Asian markets, with a deep understanding of local policies and cultural requirements.
  • Integrated Service Capability: Capable of providing one-stop integrated solutions from R&D and production, system integration, to logistics, transportation, and after-sales support.
  • Compliance and Sustainable Development: Strictly adheres to international defense trade regulations and regional laws, and is dedicated to implementing the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Future Vision

GTI will continue to expand into ASEAN and global markets, solidifying its competitive edge in the fields of defense equipment, simulation technology, and mineral resource development. Through continuous technological upgrading, regional cooperation, and talent development, GTI aims to become a significant force in promoting Laos's industrial modernization and regional economic cooperation, striving to build a model technology enterprise rooted locally with a global reach.
